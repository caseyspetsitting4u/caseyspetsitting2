Convenient Care for Busy Lives

Life gets busy—but your pet’s care shouldn’t suffer because of it. Whether you're working a long shift, heading out for the evening, or taking a weekend trip, Casey's Pet Sitting 4 U offers convenient drop-in visits to make sure your pets are happy, healthy, and looked after.

Our drop-in service is ideal for both dogs and cats, providing comfort and companionship while you're away—without disrupting their routine.

During each visit, we can provide:

  • Fresh food and water
  • Potty breaks or litter box cleaning
  • Dog walks or playtime
  • Medication (if needed)
  • Cuddles, belly rubs, or quiet company—whatever your pet prefers

Each drop-in visit is tailored to your pet’s unique needs. Whether they crave attention or simply need a check-in, you can count on us to show up with care and consistency.

Reliable. Professional. Loving. That’s Casey’s Pet Sitting 4 U.
